What is CPC?

CPC Stands for? Cost Per Click? and is used in online advertising. CPC defines how much revenue a publisher receives each time a user clicks an advertisement link on his/her website. For example, a publisher may place text or image-based ads on his/her website. When a visitor clicks one of the advertisements, he or she is directed to the advertiser's website. Each click is recorded by the advertiser's tracking system and the publisher is paid a certain amount based on the CPC.

What Is a Keyword?
